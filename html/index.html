Open Access & Transformative Agreement Finder

This site helps Northwestern-affiliated authors on the Evanston and Chicago campuses find journals where Article Processing Charges (APCs) are waived or discounted through open access (OA) and transformative agreements (TA) negotiated by Northwestern University Libraries and the Big Ten Academic Alliance (BTAA).

Considerations:

  • The corresponding author must be affiliated with Northwestern University. Confirm eligibility and covered article types, follow each journal's submission guidelines, and use your Northwestern email address when submitting to avoid verification delays.
  • If a journal is not listed, it may still be open access or covered by a separate agreement, a departmental membership, or a publisher-wide deal where individual titles are not enumerated here. For some publishers (e.g., Springer Nature, Wiley, ACS, Cambridge, IOP), coverage is listed at the publisher level — use the publisher's own journal finder to confirm a specific title.
  • When searching common terms in titles, the results list can be long. Try searching by eISSN for a direct match, or adjust 'Items per page' to see more titles at once.

See Open Access Publishing at Northwestern for full agreement details, or email scholarlycommunication@northwestern.edu with questions.

EISSNs link to supplemental information, including associated ISSNs under “Identifiers” and links to journal websites (URLs) under “Links” on the ISSN portal.

Discount or Waiver Amount may vary by article type. Check the agreement info for details.

Coverage Years generally apply to the article's acceptance date, though some publishers may use the submission date.
